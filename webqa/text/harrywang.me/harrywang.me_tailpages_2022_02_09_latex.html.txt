Latex support is based on MathJax.
Enclose the LaTex formula in $$...$$. Some examples:



\(y^{x}\)
y^{x}
\(y_{x}\)
y_{x}


\(\frac{x}{y}\)
\frac{x}{y}
\(\sum_{k=1}^n\)
\sum_{k=1}^n


\(\sqrt[n]{x}\)
\sqrt[n]{x}
\(\prod_{k=1}^n\)
\prod_{k=1}^n
